CONCLUSION

Based on the survey data, a total of 50 people were surveyed. Out of them, 35 people (70%) are eligible for the Raithu Bharosa Scheme, while 15 people (30%) are not eligible.

This indicates that a significant majority of the surveyed population qualifies for the scheme, suggesting that it is benefiting a large portion of the targeted group. Further analysis could be done to understand the reasons behind ineligibility for the remaining individuals.
