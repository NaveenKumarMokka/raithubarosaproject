✅ Raithu Bharosa Scheme – Project Abstract

  • The Raithu Bharosa Scheme is a government initiative aimed at providing financial support to eligible farmers.
  • The scheme is designed to enhance agricultural productivity and promote socio-economic welfare in rural areas.
  • This project focuses on systematically identifying and verifying individuals eligible for the scheme.
  • The process includes data collection, analysis, and validation using both manual and digital methods.
  • Verification criteria involve:
    • Land ownership records
    • Crop cultivation details
    • Socio-economic status
    • Aadhaar-linked identity confirmation
  • A major goal is to eliminate ineligible beneficiaries to maintain transparency and ensure fair allocation of funds.
  • Emphasis is placed on building a technology-driven identification system that ensures accuracy and efficiency.
  • The outcome is a streamlined, reliable method of distributing benefits directly to genuine farmers.
  • Ultimately, the project aims to support sustainable agricultural development and empower rural communities.
